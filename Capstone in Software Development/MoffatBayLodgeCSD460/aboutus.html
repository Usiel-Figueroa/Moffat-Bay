About Moffat Bay Lodge

Nestled along the pristine shores of Joviedsa Island in Washington State, Moffat Bay Lodge is a premier waterfront retreat designed to showcase the natural beauty and cultural heritage of the San Juan Islands. Just six months ago, the San Juan Islands First Nations Development Committee approved the vision for a world-class resort at Moffat Bay, bringing together traditional stewardship of the land with modern guest experiences.

Construction of the lodge is now nearing completion, with the goal of creating a sanctuary where guests can relax, recharge, and connect with nature. Surrounded by breathtaking views of the Pacific Northwest, the resort offers thoughtfully designed accommodations, locally inspired dining, and a host of activities that celebrate the island’s unique character.

At Moffat Bay Lodge, our mission is to provide more than just a vacation — we deliver a meaningful escape. Guests can look forward to evenings by the water, guided cultural experiences, and unparalleled hospitality that reflects the spirit of the island. Whether you are seeking tranquility, adventure, or a bit of both, Moffat Bay Lodge promises an unforgettable stay.

Our Luxury Rooms

Each of our rooms has been carefully designed to be one-of-a-kind, offering a unique experience that blends luxury with comfort. From handcrafted furniture to island-inspired décor, every detail reflects the natural surroundings of Joviedsa Island. Whether you choose a cozy waterfront suite or a spacious king room, you’ll enjoy elegance, modern amenities, and a style all your own.

Island Activities

Your adventure begins at Moffat Bay. Guests can enjoy a wide variety of outdoor activities designed to make the most of the island’s pristine environment:

  • Hiking – Explore scenic trails with breathtaking coastal and forest views.
  • Kayaking – Paddle along calm bay waters and discover hidden coves.
  • Whale Watching – Experience the thrill of seeing orcas and other marine life.
  • Scuba Diving – Dive beneath the surface and explore vibrant underwater ecosystems.

Whether you are seeking peaceful exploration or thrilling adventure, the island offers something for every type of traveler.
